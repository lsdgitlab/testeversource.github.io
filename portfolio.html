Ayana Renewable Power Pvt. Ltd.

Ayana Renewable Power Private Limited (“Ayana”) was incorporated in 2017 to develop utility-scale renewable energy generation projects across India. With a target to build 10 GW of renewable energy capacity by 2025, Ayana is well placed to play an essential role in India’s ambition to build 500 GW of non-fossil fuel capacity by 2030. Ayana plans to achieve its platform targets through greenfield development and value accretive acquisitions for rapid scale-up. In doing so, it will help meet the objective of ensuring transition to low carbon power generation, creating job opportunities in the renewable power sector and contributing towards the development of communities near locations where its power projects will be set up.
For more information, please visit www.ayanapower.com

Radiance Renewables Pvt. Ltd.

Radiance Renewables Private Limited ("Radiance") develops, owns, and operates distributed renewable energy generation projects (rooftop, ground-mounted, behind the meter, and open access) to supply energy to Commercial & Industrial ("C&I") customers. Incorporated in 2018 by Eversource Capital, Radiance aims to build a trusted brand with a low cost of capital by leveraging its existing Pan-India customer base and global relationships. The Company is targeting to build a >2.5 GW renewable energy generation platform by 2025.

Radiance is 100% owned by Eversource Capital.

For more information, please visit www.radiancerenewables.com

Kathari Water Management Pvt. Ltd.

Kathari Water Management Private Limited (“Kathari”) is a platform founded by Eversource Capital in 2021 with an aim to own and operate assets in water and wastewater related subsectors in India, including sewage treatment plants, desalination, effluent treatment, and O&M etc. With the Government launching initiatives such as Jal Jeevan Mission, AMRUT Mission, Namami Gange, and Smart Cities, etc. aiming to provide water to every household in India, treat the sewage system, and purify existing water bodies, Kathari is well positioned to tap into this opportunity and generate sustainable returns.

Kathari is 100% owned by Eversource Capital.

For more information, please visit www.kathariwater.com

GreenCell Mobility Pvt. Ltd.

Incorporated in 2019 by Eversource Capital, GreenCell Mobility Private Limited (“GreenCell”) is a leading provider of end-to-end, green Mobility as a Service ("MaaS") in India. GreenCell targets to become India’s largest e-Mobility as a Service (“e-MaaS”) platform by 2025 with more than 5,000 buses under management. GreenCell will be an integrated infrastructure provider with the largest network of fast chargers and depots for e-buses. GreenCell is also building a customer facing brand synonymous with reliability, premium services, and green transportation.

GreenCell is 100% owned by Eversource Capital.

For more information, please visit www.greencellmobility.com

Lithium Urban Technologies

Founded in 2014, Lithium Urban Technologies Private Limited is India’s largest EV fleet operator providing Mobility as a Service ("MaaS") in India. It is currently focused on the 4-wheeler employee transportation and 3-wheeler freight segments where it is catalysing the adoption of electric vehicles through an integrated technology-enabled service offering, long-term relationships with its stakeholders and differentiated financing solutions.

The Company serves marquee clients like SAP, Google, Microsoft etc.

Eversource Capital invested in Lithium in March 2022 and owns a 76.93% stake in the Company.
